Grab a cup of tea and let me tell you about myself

I’m a historian turned techie. I recently graduated with a Master’s degree in History. For the longest time I wanted to pursue a career in Academia (Professor McGonnagal might have had something to do with that…).

Throughout my last year at University I discovered that I was more and more drawn to alternative career paths. I kept reading about coding bootcamps, other people’s career transitions, listened to tech podcasts religiously. In December 2019 I finally made the jump.

I enrolled in Skillcrush’s ‘Break into tech’ blueprint and discovered the world of tech. And coding. And web design.

I slowly discovered my love for creating — mocking up ideas and designs and turning them into beautiful, functional websites.

My technical skills
Today I offer web design and development for busy and passionate women entrepreneurs and women-owned businesses. My current but growing skillset:

  1. HTML
  2. CSS
  3. Photoshop
  4. Web Design

My previous work experiences
When I'm not designing and building websites I work at Frankfurt University, Germany, where I help students reach their university-related or professional goals and teach in-person workshops on time management, productivity, goal-setting and motivation.

I also oversee a team of several student assistants and Peer-Coaches, manage day to day operations and communications, and design and develop digital instructional materials for self-study purposes.

What I am passionate about
I'm a strong believer in continuous learning and curiosity. I'm convinced that we can find answers to most questions through research and perseverance. Already throughout my studies I developed a passion for problem-solving. If you encounter an issue or a problem with your website, I will use all my creativity to figuring it out for you.
